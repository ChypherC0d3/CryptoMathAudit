========================================
CONCLUSION
========================================

No differential bug found with these 1300 valid vectors.

Both the reference and AVX512 backends accept all 1300 valid signatures
identically. The field arithmetic boundary vectors (limb carry, high/low
bits near p and 0) did not trigger any divergence.

This does NOT rule out bugs -- the AVX512 field arithmetic could still
diverge on:
  - Invalid/malformed signatures (reject vs accept divergence)
  - Specific bit patterns not covered by these categories
  - Edge cases in the group equation check (not just decompression)
  - Batch verification paths
