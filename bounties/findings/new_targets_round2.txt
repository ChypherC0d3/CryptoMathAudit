KEY OBSERVATIONS
================================================================================

1. FROST/Schnorr threshold signatures (Serai, Stacks) are underserved --
   fewer researchers audit this compared to ECDSA or BLS.

2. STARK/FRI systems (SP1, RISC Zero, ZKsync OS) have had recent soundness
   bugs -- this is a fertile area, especially Fiat-Shamir domain separation.

3. Multi-proof verifiers (zkVerify) are new and combine multiple attack
   surfaces in one codebase -- integration bugs are likely.

4. The BLS signature space (EigenLayer) has the highest bounties but also
   the most competition from researchers.

5. Rust-only targets have significantly fewer competing researchers than
   Solidity-heavy programs.

6. Programs with scope updates in 2025-2026 (Serai, Stacks, zkVerify) have
   the freshest code and highest probability of undiscovered bugs.
